[h1][b]Stellar Expansion is NOT required.[/b][/h1]
It is simply compatible with this mod and designed to work alongside it.

This is a simple addon mod which adds a couple of technologies that play with Robotic Modification, adding some trait points, and reducing robomodification cost.

[h1][b]Features[/b][/h1]
[list]
	[*]Repeatable technology which adds Trait Points for the purposes of Robomodification. (Up to 5 total.)
	[*]Repeatable technology which reduces the cost of Robomodification and increases Engineering Research Speed.
[/list]
