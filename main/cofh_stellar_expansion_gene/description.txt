[h1][b]Stellar Expansion is NOT required.[/b][/h1]
It is simply compatible with this mod and designed to work alongside it.

This is a simple addon mod which plays with genetic manipulation, adding some trait points.

[h1][b]Features[/b][/h1]
[list]
	[*]Repeatable technology which adds Trait Points for the purposes of genetic manipulation. (Up to 5 total.)
[/list]
