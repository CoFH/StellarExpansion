[h1][b]Stellar Expansion is NOT required.[/b][/h1]
It is simply compatible with this mod and designed to work alongside it.

This mod adds a couple of new techs and expands the orbital terraforming options.

[h1][b]Features[/b][/h1]
[list]
	[*]Terraformation of Toxic worlds into Continental, Ocean, and Tropical worlds.
	[*]Terraformation of Barren worlds into Desert, Arid, and Savanna worlds.
	[*]Terraformation of Cold Barren worlds into Arctic, Tundra, and Alpine worlds.
[/list]

If you also want to terraform Frozen and Molten worlds, then you need the following file as well: [b][i][url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=733386004]Stellar Expansion - Terraforming (Extreme)[/url][/i][/b]
