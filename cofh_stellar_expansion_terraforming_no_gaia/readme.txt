Society
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Atmospheric Purification:
	New Worlds

	Prerequisites:
		Tech - Atmospheric Manipulation

	Effects:
		Unlocks - Mechanic: Atmospheric Purification
			Allows Tomb and Toxic worlds to be terraformed:
				Continental: 3 Liquid, 3 Gas
				Ocean: 4 Liquid, 2 Gas
				Jungle: 2 Liquid, 4 Gas

Habitable World Terraformation:
	New Worlds

	Prerequisites:
		Tech - Atmospheric Manipulation

	Effects:
		Unlocks - Mechanic: Habitable World Terraformation
			Allows Barren and Cold Barren worlds to be terraformed:
				Barren -> Arid: 3 Liquid, 3 Gas
				Barren -> Desert: 2 Liquid, 4 Gas
				Cold Barren -> Arctic: 4 Liquid, 2 Gas
				Cold Barren -> Tundra: 3 Liquid, 3 Gas
